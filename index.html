About Me

I am a PhD researcher at RWTH Aachen University and Forschungszentrum Jülich, Germany. I received my M.Eng. in AI Software from Gachon University, South Korea, and my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Daffodil International University, Bangladesh. My research focuses on neuromorphic vision, object detection, hybrid SNN-ANN systems, and transformer-based learning techniques.

Research Interests

  • Neuromorphic Computer Vision
  • Hybrid SNN-ANN Architectures
  • Event-based Cameras
  • Object Detection & 3D Perception
  • Transformers and Attention Mechanisms
  • Self-supervised & Unsupervised Learning
