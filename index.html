About Me

Soikat Hasan Ahmed is a PhD researcher at RWTH Aachen University and Forschungszentrum Jülich, Germany. He holds an M.Eng. from Gachon University, South Korea, and a B.Sc. from Daffodil International University, Bangladesh. His research focuses on neuromorphic vision, object detection, hybrid SNN-ANN systems, and transformer-based learning.

Research Interests

  • Neuromorphic Computer Vision
  • Hybrid SNN-ANN Architectures
  • Event-based Cameras
  • 3D Perception & Object Detection

  • IEEE TCSVT 2022: Unsupervised deep event stereo for depth estimation – S.H. Ahmed et al.
  • USTA 2020: Low-cost semi-automated wheelchair prototype – S.H. Ahmed et al.
